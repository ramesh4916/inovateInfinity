About Dr. Srinivasa Reddy

  • Dr. Srinivasa Reddy has 28 years of prominent experience as an Interventional Cardiologist.
  • He has extensive experience in performing more than 1,150 PTCA cases and 6,200 Coronary Angiograms.
  • His area of specialization is Coronary Angiograms and Coronary Interventions, Trans-esophageal ECHO and Temporary Pacemaker.
  • He is a member of the Andhra Pradesh Medical Council, the Indian Medical Association, and the Association of Surgeons of India.
  • Dr. Srinivasa Reddy earned his MBBS (1991) from the M S Ramaiah Medical College, Bangalore.
  • He completed his MS (1998) at Kuvempu University, Karnataka.
  • Dr. Srinivasa Reddy received his DM (2003) from Dr. NTR University of Health Sciences, Andhra Pradesh.
