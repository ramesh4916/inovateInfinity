About Dr Revanth Gangasani Reddy

  • Dr. Revanth Gangasani Reddy is a leading Surgical Oncologist with over 10 years of expertise.
  • His area of specialization is Head and neck cancer surgeries, Breast cancer surgeries, Thoracic cancer surgeries, Gastrointestinal cancer surgeries, Genitourinary surgeries, and Gynecological surgeries.
  • He is a member of the Andhra Pradesh Medical Council, the Indian Medical Association, the Association of Surgeons of India, the Indian Association of Surgical Oncology, and the American Society of Clinical Oncology.
  • Dr Revanth Gangasani Reddy acquired his MBBS (2007) and MS (2012) from Dr. NTR University of Health Sciences, Andhra Pradesh.
  • He obtained his MCh (2016) from the Sri Venkateswara Institute of Medical Sciences, Tirupathi.
